4. Privacy Notice

Your personal information will be collected by Health Gateway (Ministry of Health) and Service BC under the authority of section 26(c) of the Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act for the purpose of providing access to your health records. 5. Privacy Protection Guidance for Minors

The below guidance is recommended for anyone under the age of 19.

Health Gateway takes your privacy seriously. Although Health Gateway includes security features, you must also take steps to protect your privacy:

  1. Do not set up a Health Gateway account if you cannot fully control who has access to your device. If you share your phone or password with others, the security of your information is at risk. Anyone who has access to your device may be able to open your Health Gateway account and see your:

    • prescriptions, such as birth control medication,

    • health visit and procedure details,

    • vaccination status, and

    • blood test results, including sexually transmitted infection testing.

  2. If you can’t use Health Gateway because you don’t have your own secure device, we can tell you about other ways to access the health information you need. Email us at: HealthGateway@gov.bc.ca

  3. Before setting up a Health Gateway account, make sure you have a password on your device that only you know. For tips on how to create a secure password, visit https://www.getcybersafe.gc.ca/en/secure-your-accounts/passphrases-passwords-and-pins. Or you can secure your device with facial recognition or fingerprint identification.

  4. Make sure to log out of your account and close the screen once you are finished using Health Gateway. Lock and store your device securely when not in use.

  5. If you suspect your personal information has been viewed without your permission, please reach out to a trusted adult for help on what to do next. For more information, adults may go to https://www.priv.gc.ca/en/privacy-topics/information-and-advice-for-individuals/privacy-and-kids/.

For more tips on how to protect your privacy, visit: https://www.getcybersafe.gc.ca

If you notice anything unusual in your account or if you have any privacy questions, contact the Health Gateway team for help: HealthGateway@gov.bc.ca
